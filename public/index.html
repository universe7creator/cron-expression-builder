Frequently Asked Questions

What cron formats are supported?

Standard Unix cron with 5 fields (minute hour day month weekday) and extended 6-field format. Supports special characters like * (any), / (step), - (range), , (list), L (last), and W (weekday).

Yes! With full API access, you can validate cron expressions programmatically. Perfect for pre-deployment validation of scheduled jobs.
